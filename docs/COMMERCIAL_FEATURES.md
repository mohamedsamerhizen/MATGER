# Commercial Feature Map

MATGER is designed to look and behave like a serious e-commerce backend engine.

## Catalog Presentation

- Brands.
- Product images.
- Product specifications.
- Featured products.
- New arrivals.
- Search/filter/sort/pagination.

## Pricing

- Cost price.
- Cost snapshot at checkout.
- Sale windows.
- Effective price.
- Price history.
- Profit reports.

## Inventory and Fulfillment

- Inventory movements.
- Inventory reservations.
- Expired reservation cleanup.
- Reorder planning.
- Stock adjustment approval workflow.
- Picking lists.
- CSV order export.

## Customer Operations

- Customer 360 profile.
- Customer segmentation.
- Customer internal notes.
- Wishlist.
- Reviews and moderation.

## Financial/Retention

- Wallet/store credit.
- Wallet transactions.
- Loyalty points.
- Loyalty transactions.
- Admin loyalty summary.

## Risk and Admin Intelligence

- Risk signals.
- Risk summary.
- Operations dashboard.
- Sales overview.
- Inventory overview.
- Profit reports.
